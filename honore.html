5 years of serving communities

Honore Bakery started with a dream of flying through the night sky, but instead of seeing stars, the sky was illuminated with glorious pastries of every kind. Croissants, danishes, pain aux chocalat, dotted the night sky as the aroma of freshly baked viennoiseries wafted through the air. And thus, Honore was born.

Once you enter, the cozy atmosphere surrounds you like a gentle hug. You experience joy and satisfaction with each bite of their delicious food and beverages.

Jojo Aldecoa, founder of Honore Bakery, began his passion for baking in high school. He went on to pursue culinary arts at the De La Salle College of Saint Benilde.

Now with a bakery of his own, he shares his passion and knowledge through his freshly becked creations. In the future, he hopes to study in Le Cordon Bleu, Paris, to bring a taste of Paris to Kalibo.
